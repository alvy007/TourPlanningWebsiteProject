Sonargaon Travel Guide: Visiting Museam And Other Attraction.

Sonargaon is a short drive from the bustling capital city. You can easily reach it by car, ride-sharing apps, or public transport. The journey itself is scenic, offering glimpses of rural Bangladesh with lush green fields, ponds, and vibrant local markets along the way.

Sonargaon MUSEAM, DHAKA.

Handicrafts Galore: From intricately woven nakshi kantha (embroidered quilts) to colorful clay dolls, the museum's collection showcases the unparalleled craftsmanship of Bengali artisans.

Musical Instruments: Discover a variety of traditional instruments like the ektara, dotara, and dhol, which are integral to Bengali folk music.

Historical Artifacts: The museum also displays ancient coins, pottery, and household items that provide a glimpse into the daily lives of people from centuries past.

Textile Heritage: Don't miss the exhibits on Jamdani weaving, a UNESCO-recognized intangible cultural heritage. You can even watch artisans demonstrate the intricate process of creating these exquisite sarees.
